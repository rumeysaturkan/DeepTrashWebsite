About Us

In today’s world, underwater pollution poses a great threat to the health of our lives in the underwater world. Our rivers and streams, which are filled with plastic and a lot of other garbage, are transported to the seas and oceans. DeepTrash is aimed to make an accelerating contribution by providing visual support to the cleaning and analysis studies carried out in this area with the detection of underwater waste, and to create public awareness for underwater lives affected by underwater pollution.

  • Object Detection

    By uploading underwater video or images files with our model that we have trained with underwater images, ensuring that it can detect trash, bio and rovs in the relevant file.

  • Awareness

    What we humans do to the oceans determines the future of all life as we know it. To raise awareness by raising awareness of people with news about marine pollution and detections of submarine pollution.
